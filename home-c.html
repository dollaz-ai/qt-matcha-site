the iron story

matcha had an iron problem. we designed around it.

Iron deficiency is one of the most common nutrient shortfalls in the US — especially for women, athletes, and anyone plant-based. And here's the twist: the tannins in regular tea can actually make it harder for your body to absorb iron.

So we rebuilt matcha from the ground up: an iron-conscious blend with added vitamin C to support absorption, timed so your daily cup works with your body, not against it.
